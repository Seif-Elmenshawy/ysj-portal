                     YSJ 2026: Juniors Yeah!!!!!
                     Description:

                     YSJ's Junior Research Program is a student research program designed to help high school
                     students learn research from choosing a research question to publishing a full paper and become
                     part of a community of passionate researchers.
                     Throughout the program, participants will have opportunities to:

                          • Explore the foundations of academic research
                          • Learn how researchers investigate questions and evaluate evidence
                          • Engage with peers who share similar academic interests

                     Selection Criteria
                     YSJ seeks Juniors with strong potential for growth rather than extensive prior achievements.
                     When reviewing applications, we place particular emphasis on:

                          • Intellectual curiosity
                          • Initiative and self-motivation
                          • Commitment to learning
                          • Alignment with the values of the program

                     Previous research experience is not required.


                     Application Process
                     The selection process consists of the following stages:

                          • Stage 1: Application Review
                            Applicants complete the application form, including written responses designed to help us
                            understand their personalities and experiences.
                          • Stage 2: Interview
                            Selected applicants will be invited to an interview where they will discuss their applications.
                          • Stage 3: Final Decisions
                            Successful applicants will receive an admission decision following the completion of the
                            selection process.

                     Terms of Agreement

                          • Juniors should participate actively in program activities.
                          • Juniors are expected to be open to feedback from the YSJ Seniors.
                          • Juniors are required to demonstrate professionalism and respectful communication.
                          • Juniors should complete assigned tasks and responsibilities on time.
                          • Juniors are required to demonstrate willingness to collaborate within a team and genuine
                            commitment to the journal's mission.
